Who I Am

About Me

Professionally rigorous. Personally grounded. Always curious.

I am a Technical Product Manager and Business Analyst with 8.5+ years of experience turning complex, ambiguous problems into clear, scalable solutions. What sets me apart is that I am genuinely domain-agnostic — I love deep diving into new industries, learning the language of the domain, and understanding user needs from first principles before building anything.

Whether it is redesigning a fulfilment platform that moved ₹30 crore of medicines across India every month, building a portable spirometry device from ground zero that put real-time pulmonary diagnostics into the hands of doctors, or launching a civic complaint platform that gave Mumbai citizens a direct voice to their BMC ward — I care about solving problems that make a real difference in people's lives.

My approach is structured and evidence-driven, shaped by years across regulated industries — banking, healthcare, and fintech — where rigour and empathy must coexist. I bring the same mindset to every new problem: understand the user deeply, define the problem precisely, and build iteratively with data as the guide.

Beyond work, I am an avid reader, a passionate animal lover, and I dream of one day contributing meaningfully to the welfare of stray animals. Dance is my meditation — my way of resetting and finding joy. And lifting weights is where I release the world's weight back onto the bar.

I also draw quietly from philosophy — the Bhagavad Gita's principle of purposeful, detached action informs how I approach product decisions and long-term thinking more than most frameworks ever could.

Interview Case Study

Spocto X · NBFC Integration

A senior BA / Pre-Sales case study submitted as part of a multi-round interview at Spocto X (Yubi Group). The brief was to act as Implementation Lead for a fintech collections platform integration with a mid-sized NBFC — from Day 1 to Go-Live in 8 weeks.
The Brief

Background: SpoctoX is an AI-powered collections intelligence platform used by banks, NBFCs, and HFCs to manage delinquent loan portfolios — covering digital communication (SMS, WhatsApp, Email, Voice Bot), field agent management, telecalling, and analytics dashboards.

Assignment: Assigned as Implementation Lead for FinServe Capital — a mid-sized NBFC with ~2 lakh active loan accounts across personal loans, two-wheeler loans, and microfinance across 12 states. Day 1 of the project. Signed contract in hand. Go-live target: 8 weeks.

Constraints Given
  • · Lean IT team — 3 people, already on another migration
  • · Business owner has limited bandwidth; wants minimal meetings
  • · CBS integration specs not yet discussed
  • · WhatsApp + SMS required live on Day 1 of go-live
  • · No existing documentation of current collections workflow
Deliverables Required
  • · Part 1 — 8-week Project Plan with phases, milestones, risks
  • · Part 2 — Structured BA Discovery Questionnaire (15–20 questions)
  • · Part 3 — Top 5 Risk & Issue Log with mitigation and ownership
  • · Part 4 — Kick-off email to FinServe's collections ops head
My Approach — Going Beyond the Ask

Rather than answering only the four parts, I structured my submission around a full Implementation Lead methodology — conducting domain research first to understand NBFC ecosystems, CBS/LMS architecture, RBI regulatory frameworks, and DPD-based collections workflows before designing any artefact.
